Technology Research & Key Insights

Where to find reliable information on technology and scientific innovation

Information about technology research and modern innovations can be found across various trusted platforms and publications. Below are some of the most notable sources for staying updated on advancements in science and technology.

Google Research

Google Research publishes papers and findings across a wide range of domains, including AI/ML foundations, responsible human‑centric technology, and new computing paradigms. Its mission is to drive breakthroughs that benefit society and businesses while advancing technological capabilities.

Impact of Science and Technology

Innovations in communication technology and medicine have transformed how people connect and receive care. Technology has extended lifespans, improved healthcare systems, and enhanced overall quality of life through continuous advancements.

Science & Technology Blogs

Numerous blogs highlight the latest scientific discoveries and tech innovations. Some well‑known examples include:

  • Live Science — Covers scientific discoveries, space, physics, and more.
  • PLOS — Features open‑access scientific studies across multiple disciplines.
  • The New York Times' BITS Blog — Focuses on technological innovations from smartphones to big data.
