About this event



Inauguration of Asia's First Liquid Galaxy Lab at VIT Pune

It is with great pride that we announce the inauguration of Google's very own Liquid Galaxy Lab, setting up in Asia for the first time,
         hosted here at our VIT Pune campus, Led by Department of CSE (AI & ML).
This is a landmark event for our institution, as we join a select group of labs, becoming the 5th alongside those in
 Lleida (Spain) - Liquid Galaxy project open-source world headquarters, New York (USA), Facens (Brazil), and Sydney (Australia)!

his landmark event will feature insightful talks and live demonstrations by prominent tech leaders, 
    including Google Developer Expert (GDE)x2 Web and Google Maps Yash Raj Bharti, community leader Vedant Singh,
     and Andreu Ibañez, global admin of the project.
     Engage in enlightening discussions about open-source technologies and witness firsthand how they are transforming the global tech landscape.

Attendees will have the unique opportunity to experience the immersive capabilities of the Liquid Galaxy system through live demos 
        and learn how to contribute to these cutting-edge open-source projects.
         Whether you are a seasoned developer or just starting your journey, this event offers something for everyone passionate about technology.

Don't miss out on this exciting kickoff for a facility that puts VIT Pune on the global map! Be there to connect, learn, and grow.

RSVP now and be a part of our thriving tech community! We look forward to welcoming you.
